a little about me

visual artist & ui/ux designer

sadie mothershed

Throughout the years, I have had wide variety of interests, ranging from fine arts to forensic sciences to space exploration. The one thing all of them have in common is they exist as means to to make people's lives easier.

As a student at Georgia Tech, emphasis on innovation has allowed me to refine my goal of ''improving the user experience'' into bridging the gap between working hard and enjoying work in a way that boosts productivity. I want people to feel satisfied with what they're doing in life by making everyday tasks feel like less of a burden.
